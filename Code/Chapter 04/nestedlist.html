• How to make tea:
    1. Boil water. Take 3/4th cup for every cup you want to make.
    2. Now add tea powder according to number of cups. You can take one teaspoon per cup.
    3. Now add sugar as per your taste buds.
    4. Bring the mixture to a boil.
    5. Sieve and pour in cups.
    6. Add warm milk for milk tea.
  • How to make coffee:
    1. Heat up the water and get a mug and put in two tablespoons of sugar and a teaspoon of instant coffee.
    2. When the water is ready, pour in as much to fill the cup, allowing for any liquid you plan to add.
    3. Add creamer to the coffee and stir.
